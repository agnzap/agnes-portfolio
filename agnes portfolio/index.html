Frequently Asked Questions

#1 Why did you decide to become a web developer?

Because I wanted to be able to create awesome websites without relying on anyone else;)
Also because it always seemed a fun and useful skill to have.

#2 What is your design proces?

The whole process begins and ends with you - my customer. The first meetings are crucial, since this is when we establish what are your goals and expectations, your likes and dislikes. At the beginning we need to determine what the website should entail, i.e. come up with the site architecture. As the next step, it is useful to create a moodboard so that I get to know more about your taste, color preferences etc. Once we are done with all these points, the real fun begins - I get to sit down and work on your website using all the knowledge and tools I have. When it is done, we sit together again, I walk you through the finished project. This is the right time to let me know if there's anything you do not like ;)

If we're clear, the website can go online!

#3 What is your average project turnaround?

Unfortunately - it depends. It depends on how complicated the site's architectures is, on any "extra" features the client requested. On top of that, it depends on how many other projects I currently have on my plate. Assuming I'm free as a bird and the website you want me to build is pretty simple (up to 4 subpages, no fireworks), I will need about 2 weeks to finish the project.
